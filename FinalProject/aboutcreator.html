About the creator

My name is Janet YiTing Sun and I am a second year student in the Computation Art program at Concordia University. I am also currently a tattoo apprentice at Enigma Tattoo in Montreal.

I have always been passionate about concept art and game design. There is no limit to an artist's imagination and I was fascinated by the art of game making.

After graduating from Art, Communication and Literature program in Dawson College, I decided to continue my post-secondary education at Concordia University to persue my dream career.
